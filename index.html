What Linux distro do I use?

You probably didn't ask this but I use Pop!_OS. After a good while of distro hopping (the act of changing linux distributions frequently) I decided that Pop!_OS provided me with the greatest stability out of the box and also since It's based on Ubuntu it has a large amount of support. I also really appreciate the System76 company, they have consistently made quality products such as their keyboard and computers.
